Welcome to Myrtle Beach!

My favorite vacation spot is Myrtle Beach. The beach has opportunities for everyone. I prefer to sit by the water or on a balcony with a book, reading with the ocean breeze directly in my face. There is also a great seafood buffet that I go to every time I'm there. Because MBSC is such a popular destination, there are a number of tourist-friendly attractions mainly in South Myrtle Beach, even some that you can walk to from the long string of beachfront resorts.

Things to do!

  • For adults
    • Banana boating
    • Minigolf
    • Captain George's Seafood Buffet
    • Polynesian Fire Luau (yes, this is real)
  • For families
    • Sand castles
    • Ripley's Aquarium
    • Alligator Adventure (reptile zoo)
    • Swimming, if you're brave
